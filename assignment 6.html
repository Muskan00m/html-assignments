HTML Article

The article tag specifies independent, self-contained content. An article should make sense on its own and it should be possible to distribute it independently from the rest of the site. Potential sources for the articleelement:

  • Forum post
  • Blog post
  • News story
Note: The article element does not render as anything special in a browser. However, you can use CSS to style the article element (see example below).
